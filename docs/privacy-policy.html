Privacy Policy for fastwin

Last updated: June 3, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how fastwin ("the app", "we", "our", or "us") handles information when you use the Android application with package name com.striklewin.apps.

Information We Collect

The native game part of fastwin does not require an account and does not ask you to provide your name, email address, phone number, precise location, or other direct personal identifiers.

The app stores gameplay information locally on your device, such as your high score and app preferences. This information is used only to keep the app working as expected and is not sent to our own server.

Remote Configuration and Firebase

The app uses Firebase Firestore to read a remote configuration value from the collection config, document app, field url. This value is used to decide whether the app should open the native game experience or a fullscreen WebView.

When the app contacts Firebase, Google/Firebase may process technical data such as device information, app information, IP address, network information, and diagnostics according to Google's own privacy terms.

WebView, Cookies, and Website Content

If the remote configuration provides a web URL, fastwin opens that URL in a fullscreen WebView. The WebView supports JavaScript, DOM storage, cookies, and third-party cookies so that the website can function correctly.

Websites opened inside the WebView may collect information according to their own privacy practices. We do not control the privacy practices of third-party websites shown inside the WebView.

Camera, Files, and Downloads

The app includes WebView support for file selection, file upload, downloads, and camera capture through Android's FileProvider. These features are used only when you interact with a website inside the WebView that requests file upload or camera capture.

The app does not access your camera or files in the background. Camera or file access happens only after an action you initiate and after any required Android permission flow.

How We Use Information

Information handled by the app is used to:

  • Run the game and save local gameplay progress.
  • Read remote configuration from Firebase Firestore.
  • Open WebView content when configured.
  • Support WebView features such as cookies, file upload, camera capture, and downloads.
  • Maintain app functionality, security, and reliability.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. Technical information may be processed by service providers used by the app, including Google Firebase and services used by websites opened inside the WebView.

Data Retention and Deletion

Local app data, such as high scores and cached configuration values, remains on your device until you clear the app data or uninstall the app.

You can delete local app data through Android system settings by clearing storage for fastwin or uninstalling the app.

Children's Privacy

fastwin is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information through content opened in the WebView, please contact the operator of that website or contact us through the developer contact listed on Google Play.

Security

We use reasonable technical measures provided by Android, Firebase, and GitHub Pages to operate the app and this policy page. No method of electronic transmission or storage is completely secure.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a new "Last updated" date.

Contact

For privacy questions, use the developer contact listed for fastwin in Google Play Console or contact the repository owner through GitHub: https://github.com/daryadragan95.
