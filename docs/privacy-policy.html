Privacy Policy for fastwin

Last updated: June 3, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how fastwin ("the app", "we", "our", or "us") handles information when you use the Android application with package name com.striklewin.apps.

Information We Collect

The native game part of fastwin does not require an account and does not ask you to provide your name, email address, phone number, precise location, or other direct personal identifiers.

The app stores gameplay information locally on your device, such as your high score and app preferences. This information is used only to keep the app working as expected and is not sent to our own server.

How We Use Information

Information handled by the app is used to:

  • Run the game and save local gameplay progress.
  • Maintain app functionality, security, and reliability.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. The app does not require an account and does not share your locally stored gameplay data with our own server.

Data Retention and Deletion

Local app data, such as high scores and preferences, remains on your device until you clear the app data or uninstall the app.

You can delete local app data through Android system settings by clearing storage for fastwin or uninstalling the app.

Children's Privacy

fastwin is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us through the developer contact listed on Google Play.

Security

We use reasonable technical measures provided by Android and GitHub Pages to operate the app and this policy page. No method of electronic transmission or storage is completely secure.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a new "Last updated" date.

Contact

For privacy questions, use the developer contact listed for fastwin in Google Play Console or contact the repository owner through GitHub: https://github.com/daryadragan95.
